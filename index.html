About Panchayat Season 4

Panchayat is a comedy-drama web series that follows the life of Abhishek Tripathi, a city-bred engineering graduate who reluctantly takes up a job as a Panchayat secretary in the remote village of Phulera. Season 4 continues his journey as he becomes more accustomed to rural life while dealing with the quirky villagers and their problems.
